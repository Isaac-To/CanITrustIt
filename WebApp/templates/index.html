A mission to improve the web

A new neural network trained on a diverse collection of over 13K examples of news articles both reliable and unreliable, trained to identify the trust worthiness of a site with an accuracy of over 95%.

I created this project to help improve people's access to high quality media without the biases of fake news and unreliable sources by identifying key patterns and vocabulary usages and comparing it against genuine articles that you would want to read.

Just putting this forward but as an artificial intelligence based tool, it is prone to errors and shortcomings that are inexplainable. Things like trust worthy sources may sometimes be flagged as non trustworthy and vice versa. The model isn't perfect, in fact, what counts as unreliable news and reliable is unique to each person, thus making this task extremely difficult to perfect.

To that end, be patient and let me know when things go wrong. I'm working on improving the quality of data and the training process to iron out things to the best of my ability.
